Venus :-

Venus is a planet in our solar system that is the second planet from the sun. It's the hottest planet in our solar system and is covered in a thick atmosphere. Features Atmosphere Venus's atmosphere is made up of carbon dioxide, nitrogen, and sulfuric acid. It's 90 times denser than Earth's atmosphere. Surface Venus's surface is shaped by volcanic activity. It has more volcanoes than Earth, including 167 large volcanoes that are over 60 miles across. Rotation Venus rotates clockwise on its axis, and it takes 243 Earth days to spin once. Temperature Venus's surface temperature is hot enough to melt lead. Brightness Venus is the second brightest natural object in the night sky after the Moon. Name Venus is named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty, who was also known as Aphrodite by the ancient Greeks. Other facts Venus's orbital period is 225 days.

Mars :-

Mars is the fourth planet from the sun and is known as the Red Planet. It's covered in iron oxide dust, which gives it its red color. Features Mars has two moons, Phobos and Deimos Mars has a thin atmosphere made up of carbon dioxide, nitrogen, argon, and water vapor Mars has seasons, polar ice caps, volcanoes, canyons, and weather Mars has huge dust storms that can cover the entire planet and last for months Mars is much smaller than Earth and has no deep oceans, lakes, or rivers Exploration Many spacecraft from countries including the United States, the Soviet Union, the European Space Agency, and India have visited Mars The Mars 2020 mission Perseverance rover landed on Mars in February 2021 The Curiosity rover has been investigating Mars since August 2012 to determine if it was ever habitable to microbial life Temperature Mars may look warm but it's very cold Mars is about 50 million miles farther away from the Sun than Earth, so it gets a lot less light and heat Name The ancient Romans named it Mars, who was their god of war The Babylonians named it Nergal, for their god of death and disease

Uranus :-

Uranus is an ice giant planet in our solar system that's tilted on its side and spins in the opposite direction to most other planets. It's the seventh planet from the sun and has a striking blue-green color. Features Rings: Uranus has at least 13 rings, including a narrow inner system and a more distant outer system with two brightly colored rings. Moons: Uranus has at least 27 known moons. Temperature: Uranus is the coldest planet in our solar system, with a recorded temperature of -224° C. Atmosphere: Uranus's atmosphere is made of methane, hydrogen, and helium. Tilt: Uranus's axis is tilted almost 98°, which means that the Sun points directly at each pole for long periods of time. Rotation: Uranus rotates in the opposite direction to most other planets. Composition Uranus is made up of a thick soup of water, methane, and ammonia.

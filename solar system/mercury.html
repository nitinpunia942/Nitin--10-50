Mercury :-

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and the one closest to the sun. It's a rocky planet with a rough surface, no atmosphere, and no moon. Features Size: Slightly larger than Earth's Moon Surface: Covered in craters and ridges, and a thick layer of dust Orbit: Elliptical, taking Mercury as close as 29 million miles (47 million km) and as far as 43 million miles (70 million km) from the sun Rotation: A solar day on Mercury is 59 days Orbiting period: Mercury orbits the sun every 88 Earth days Speed: Travels through space at nearly 112,000 mph (180,000 km/h), faster than any other planet Magnetic field: Has a magnetic field that interacts with the solar wind to create magnetic tornadoes Atmosphere Mercury doesn't have an atmosphere, so it's a silent world without any sound There is water in permanently shadowed craters near Mercury's poles, but it's all in the form of ice Name Mercury is named after the Roman messenger for the gods, who was known for his speed and swiftness.
